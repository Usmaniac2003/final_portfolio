About Me

I am Usman Ghani, a Software Engineer from Karachi, Pakistan, with a strong educational foundation and professional skills in full-stack development. My journey began with completing O-levels and FSc, which laid the groundwork for pursuing higher education. I then earned my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering (BS SE), which equipped me with in-depth knowledge of various aspects of software development, including algorithms, databases, and system architecture. Over time, I honed my skills in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), becoming proficient in both front-end and back-end development. As a full-stack developer, I am capable of designing and building dynamic, responsive web applications from scratch, integrating user interfaces with robust server-side functionality. My passion for software engineering drives me to continually learn and adapt to new technologies, ensuring I stay at the forefront of industry trends.
